"The forest remembers everything...

  • Enter a world where Talesville trembles, characters turn on one another, and you — the godmother with a spark of magic and wit — must uncover the truth.

Behind the Curtain

At Do Games Limited, we grew up on fairy tales — but always rooted for the misunderstood characters.
This game was designed after a debate in our team: "What if the villain was just poorly written?"
Every puzzle, character, and collectible reflects that question.
We're storytellers. We just hide the story under branches and shadows.
